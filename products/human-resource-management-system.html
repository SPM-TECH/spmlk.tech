Product Info

SPM is a software development company that provides a variety of solutions to its customers. One of its products is a Human Resource Management System (HRMS). This system is designed to help organizations effectively manage and track their human resources, which includes recruiting, hiring, and managing employees.

The HRMS from SPM Enterprise aims to provide organizations with a comprehensive solution for managing their human resources, from tracking employee information, to managing payroll and benefits, to managing employee performance. This can help organizations to improve the efficiency of their human resource processes, support compliance, and support employee development.

An HRMS is a critical tool for any organization that employs people, regardless of its size or industry. It can help companies to stay organized and on top of their workforce, which in turn can help to improve decision-making, reduce risk, and ensure compliance with regulations. While the specific features and capabilities of the SPM Enterprise HRMS may vary, the product is generally designed to provide organizations with a flexible and easy-to-use solution for managing human resources. It may include advanced features, such as automation and optimization, self-service functionality, analytics and integration capabilities.

Employee Self-Service

Many HRMS provide a self-service portal where employees can access and update their personal information, request time off, and view their pay stubs and benefits.

Recruiting and Onboarding

Many HRMS have recruiting and onboarding functionality, which allows organizations to manage the hiring process, from posting job openings to onboarding new hires.

Employee Data Management

This feature allows organizations to store and manage employee information, such as contact information, job history, performance evaluations, and more.

Payroll and Benefits Management

This feature allows organizations to manage payroll, taxes, and benefits for their employees, including tracking time-off requests, calculating and issuing paychecks, and managing benefit.

Learning Management Systems Integration

Some HRMS have the capability of integrating with LMS, which provides the ability to manage employee learning and development programs in one place.

Time and Attendance Management

Some HRMS have the capability of tracking employee time and attendance, by using time clocks, mobile app, or manual input.
